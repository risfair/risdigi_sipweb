ABOUT DIGI-SIP!

The name DIGI-SIP was originally suggested by 10-Duke's class advisor, which later won the class vote when deciding on our booth's name.

The theme was decided by the designing commitee. They thought a 2000's digital camera would be a good representation for the 5th floor theme, Y2K. The color palette of this booth was originally pink, however, the final design of blue was picked as it looked happier, and represented our theme well.

Some catch phrases in DIGI-SIP's poster - "123 FIZZ!", "Pick n' Click", "Feeling fizzy?" - were some of the other names that had been suggested by the class.

BOOTH INFO!!

Our booth consists of juice based drinks with variations of flavors. We sell drinks of two different sizes: Regular (22 oz) and Mini (18 oz), in which, once you purchase the regular sized drinks you get a chance to spin the wheel/participate in a raffle.

As digital camera's are a main part of the booth's theme, we let our buyers take photos in our photobooth.

The photos taken can be accessed via the google drive link below, or a QR code may be given.

TAKE NOTE:

You get a chance to spin the wheel ONLY if you buy the regular sized drink!!

If you do buy a certain amount of drinks, you may get a prize!
